About

Our company has created a new method to detect breast cancer using modules able to identify changes in heat across the breasts and determine if a detected difference in temperature signifies the presence of a tumor. The device is cheaper than current methods of breast cancer detection, making access to potentially life-saving technology available to a greater range of women who may need treatment.
